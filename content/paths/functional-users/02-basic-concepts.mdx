## Key Concepts

### 1. Records and Fields

Every piece of data in NetSuite lives in a **record**. Each record has:
- **Internal ID**: A unique number that never changes
- **Fields**: Specific pieces of information (like "Company Name" or "Amount")
- **Relationships**: Connections to other records

### 2. Record Types

Common record types you'll work with:
- **Customers**: People or companies that buy from you
- **Items**: Products or services you sell
- **Transactions**: Sales Orders, Invoices, Purchase Orders
- **Employees**: People who work for your company

### 3. Forms and Layouts

Forms are what you see when viewing or editing a record. They're like templates that determine:
- Which fields are shown
- How fields are arranged
- What information is required vs optional
